About Me

A little about the Past 10 years or so

Where do I begin? I graduated from Grant High School in Portland Oregon in 2011. Following graduation I picked up a job at Amalfis Italian Restaurant where I spent the last 8 years. I was always a quiet, shy kid, so going into an industry where interacting with people is a major portion of your job was a bit of a change of pace for me. It definitley changed me for the better and has made me a lot more gregarious! between working at Amalfi's I did contract painting for a few months in 2013, as well as temporary warehouse work for Kendall floral during their busy seasons between 2015 and 2016

Why Epicodus?

Back in august of 2019, My friend, Quinn, was telling me about his new job he landed at Vicasa. I was interested in how he managed to land such an awesome gig. He told me about Epicodus and how the program works. At the time, I was getting pretty tired of the late night hours and the wonky schedule that is part of working in a restaurant and was looking for a new career. After he told me what to expect and what I would need to do to succeed, I had made up my mind. later that same day I began to look into Epicodus and signed up for the Intro to Programming part time course. After about a month of the intro program I had made up my mind, i was going to enroll in the full time course. The thing that appealed to me in particular about Epicodus was their internship program, getting a foot in the door can be a major struggle for anyone looking to start a new career, and knowing that the program guarantees an internship was huge to me.
